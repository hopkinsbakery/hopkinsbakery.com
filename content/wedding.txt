<p>Hopkins Street Bakery has been making custom wedding cakes for over 20 years and has designed thousands of beautiful and delicious wedding cakes. We take pride in making your dream cake with the finest and freshest ingredients. We use <a href="http://www.guittard.com/">Guittard chocolate</a>, rBST free dairy from <a href="http://cloverstornetta.com/">Clover Stornetta Farm</a>, real vanilla and 100% butter. Our fruits are sourced locally from our neighbor <a href="http://www.montereymarket.com/">Monterey Market </a> where you are guaranteed to get the freshest fruits available. When you you order your wedding cake from Hopkins Street Bakery, you will receive one-on-one consultation from our cake chef and owner to design your dream cake for your special day. Pick from one of our popular signature cakes or custom design your own cake flavor, filling and frosting.</p>

<p>For a detailed list of custom cake and cake flavors, pricing, cake design, please download our <a href="/pdf/HopkinsWedding2009.pdf">wedding brochure</a>.</p>
